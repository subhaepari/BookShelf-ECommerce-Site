This book has continued the legacy of bringing some of the most adventurous stories for kids. In the book, protagonist Geronimo Stilton is celebrating 10 years of success in the field of writing. He wishes to celebrate his success by writing his story and publishing it as a book. But some things are not destined to happen smoothly. How is he going to type and publish his book when his precious laptop is not with him anymore? It has been stolen somewhere and nowhere to be found now. Go through the exciting tale of this laptop search and his zest to write and publish his success story.
